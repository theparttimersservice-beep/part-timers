🌾 Farming Work — ଚାଷ କାମ

  • Tractor operators must hold a valid driving licence.
  • Farm machinery insurance is strongly recommended best practice.
  • Pesticide spray workers should be certified (CIBRC guidelines).
  • Seasonal rates: The platform allows price increases during peak seasons (Kharif/Rabi).
  • The worker is not responsible for crop damage unless negligence is proven.
  • Payment should be made only after work completion — advance is optional.

🐟 Fish & Aquaculture — ମାଛ ଚାଷ

  • Follow local fish market rules for sale/purchase (Odisha Fisheries Act).
  • Inform the customer before using any pond chemicals.
  • In case of fish mortality, the worker must provide proof that it was not due to negligence.
  • Aquaculture workers should follow NFDB (National Fisheries Development Board) guidelines.

🐓 Poultry & Livestock — ପୋଲ୍ଟ୍ରି

  • Certificates from the State Veterinary Department are recommended for disease control.
  • Poultry farm workers should maintain vaccination records.
  • Animal cruelty is strictly prohibited — Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act 1960.
  • Proper vehicles are required for livestock transport (PETA guidelines).

📚 Learning & Coaching — ଶିକ୍ଷା

  • Tutors must truthfully state their qualifications — a fake degree results in a permanent ban.
  • Parent consent is mandatory for students under 18 years old.
  • Home tuition: A family member must be present — one student and one teacher alone in a single room is not permitted.
  • Platform maintains zero tolerance for child safety violations (POCSO Act 2012).
  • Obtain screen recording consent before conducting online tuition sessions.

🔧 Technicians — ଟେକ୍ନିସିଆନ

  • Electricians should have knowledge of IE Rules 1956.
  • A licensed electrician is compulsory for high-voltage work (Electricity Act 2003).
  • After completing any job, the technician should provide a completion report or WhatsApp confirmation.
  • Parts warranty: The customer should receive a receipt for all parts installed.
  • Gas fitter, plumber, and AC technician — respective trade certificate required.

🏗️ Contractors — ଠିକାଦାର

  • Construction work may require permission from local municipality or Gram Panchayat.
  • RERA (Real Estate Regulation Act 2016) applies for large projects.
  • Contractors should obtain workers' accident insurance (Workmen Compensation Act 1923).
  • Building materials should meet BIS quality standards.
  • Do not accept excessive advance before project completion — 30% maximum is recommended.

⚙️ Equipment Rental — ଯନ୍ତ୍ରପାତି ଭଡ଼ା

  • Equipment owners should maintain service and maintenance records.
  • Collecting a security deposit from the renter is allowed — a receipt is mandatory.
  • The renter is responsible for equipment damage — take before and after photos.
  • An operator licence is compulsory for heavy machinery (JCB, Crane).

🚗 Vehicle Booking — ଯାନ ବୁକିଂ

  • The driver must hold a valid licence (Motor Vehicles Act 1988).
  • Vehicle insurance is mandatory — verify that passenger insurance is also included.
  • For goods transport: vehicle fitness certificate and permit are required.
  • Drunk driving = permanent ban + police complaint.
  • For night travel (10 PM–5 AM), obtain the customer's written consent via WhatsApp.

🍽️ Food Delivery — ଖାଦ୍ୟ ଡେଲିଭରି

  • Food vendors must have FSSAI registration or licence (Food Safety & Standards Act 2006).
  • Home food makers should obtain FSSAI basic registration (free, done online).
  • Allergen disclosure is mandatory — inform about dairy, nuts, gluten, etc.
  • Food should be delivered within 2 hours, otherwise it may not be considered fresh.
  • In case of food poisoning, the vendor must provide a full refund.

🛒 Marketplace Buy/Sell — କ. ବ.

  • Sellers must accurately describe the condition of items — misleading descriptions result in a ban.
  • Second-hand electronics: A 7-day return policy is recommended.
  • Land/property listings: Verify documents first — fraud is covered under IPC 420.
  • Animal sales: Vaccination records are mandatory. Listing stolen animals is covered under IPC 379.
  • Vegetables and crops must be fresh — complaints about poor quality will be reviewed.

🏪 Sunday Haata — ରବିବାର ହାଟ

  • Only genuine local products in the Haata — fake or duplicate products are banned.
  • Respect GI (Geographical Indication) tags for handlooms — Sambalpuri and Nuapatna designs.
  • Selling wild animals or protected birds is illegal under the Wildlife Protection Act 1972.
  • Haata listings remain active for 7 days — renew afterwards.

🪪 Digital Work ID — ଡିଜିଟାଲ କାର୍ଯ୍ୟ ID

  • Work ID is only for genuine workers — it will be cancelled if misused.
  • Work ID is not a replacement for a government ID — it is platform identity proof only.
  • You may share your QR code link with anyone — but never share your account password.
⭐ Dual Rating System — ଦ୍ୱି ରେଟିଂ: Both sides can rate on this platform — Customers rate Workers and Workers rate Customers. This builds rural trust. — ଏହା ଗ୍ରାମୀଣ ବିଶ୍ୱାସ ଗଢ଼ୁଛି ।

⭐ Customer → Worker Rating

  1. After a booking is completed, the customer can give a 5-star rating.
  2. Rating scale: 1 to 5 stars — 5 is best, 1 is worst.
  3. Written feedback is optional but encouraged.
  4. Quick tags can also be used for feedback (Good work, On time, etc.).
  5. The rating will be published on the worker's public profile.
  6. Report fake or malicious ratings — they will be reviewed within 24 hours.

⭐ Worker → Customer Rating

  1. Workers can also rate customers from their orders section.
  2. Customer ratings are visible to future workers — so they know who is reliable.
  3. Worker tags: "Paid correctly", "Explained clearly", "Respectful", etc.
  4. The platform may alert workers about low-rated customers.
  5. If someone repeatedly receives ratings below 3, the platform will review the account.

📝 Feedback Guidelines

  • Feedback must not include personal attacks — only comments about the work.
  • A rating cannot be edited within 72 hours of submission.
  • Both parties can rate the same order independently.
  • Admins receive an auto-alert when any rating falls below 3 stars.
  • To report a wrong rating: send a screenshot on WhatsApp.

📊 Google Sheets Database Setup

1
Create a Google SheetOpen Google Sheets, create a new spreadsheet. Add tabs: Users, Workers, Vendors, Orders, Ratings, Ads.
2
Open Apps ScriptIn the sheet: Extensions → Apps Script. Delete old code, then paste the code from data/sheets-api.gs.
3
Deploy as Web AppDeploy → New Deployment → Web App. Set "Execute as: Me" and "Who has access: Anyone". Deploy and copy the URL.
4
Add URL to configIn data/config.js, paste your URL into the sheetsScriptUrl field. Save — done!

🗺️ Google Maps Setup

  1. Go to console.cloud.google.com → Create a new project
  2. Enable Maps JavaScript API
  3. Credentials → Create API Key → Copy it
  4. In data/config.js, paste it into mapsApiKey
  5. Restrict the key in production: add your domain under HTTP referrers
