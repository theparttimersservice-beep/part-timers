📂 Har category ke liye alag rules hain. Apni service ke section mein pado. — ପ. ବ. ।

🌾 Farming Work — ଚାଷ କାମ

  • Tractor operator ko valid driving license hona chahiye.
  • Farm machinery ka insurance hona best practice hai.
  • Pesticide spray karne wala certified hona chahiye (CIBRC guidelines).
  • Seasonal rates: Platform peak season (Kharif/Rabi) mein price hike allow karta hai.
  • Crop damage hone par worker responsible nahi — sirf negligence prove hone par.
  • Work completion pe hi payment — advance optional hai.

🐟 Fish & Aquaculture — ମାଛ ଚାଷ

  • Fish sale/purchase ke liye local fish market/mandi rules follow karo (Odisha Fisheries Act).
  • Pond chemicals use karne se pehle customer ko batao.
  • Fish mortality hone par worker ko proof dikhana hoga ki negligence nahi tha.
  • Aquaculture workers ke liye NFDB (National Fisheries Development Board) guidelines follow karni chahiye.

🐓 Poultry & Livestock — ପୋଲ୍ଟ୍ରି

  • Animal disease control ke liye state Veterinary Dept se certificates recommended.
  • Poultry farm workers ko vaccination records maintain karne chahiye.
  • Animal cruelty strictly prohibited — Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act 1960.
  • Livestock transport ke liye proper vehicle hona chahiye (PETA guidelines).

📚 Learning & Coaching — ଶିକ୍ଷା

  • Tutors ko apni qualification sach batani chahiye — fake degree = permanent ban.
  • Minor students (under 18) ke liye parent consent mandatory hai.
  • Home tuition: Family member present hona chahiye — ek student ek teacher single room mein nahi.
  • Platform child safety ke liye zero tolerance policy hai (POCSO Act 2012).
  • Online tuition ke liye screen recording consent lena chahiye.

🔧 Technicians — ଟ.

  • Electricians ko IE Rules 1956 ka knowledge hona chahiye.
  • High voltage kaam ke liye licensed electrician compulsory (Electricity Act 2003).
  • Kisi bhi kaam ke baad technician ko completion report ya WhatsApp confirmation deni chahiye.
  • Parts warranty: Jo parts lagaye unka receipt customer ko milna chahiye.
  • Gas fitter, plumber, AC technician — respective trade certificate required.

🏗️ Contractors — ଠ.

  • Construction work ke liye local municipality/gram panchayat permission zaroori ho sakti hai.
  • RERA (Real Estate Regulation Act 2016) — large projects ke liye applicable.
  • Contractor ko workers ka accident insurance lena chahiye (Workmen Compensation Act 1923).
  • Building material quality ke liye BIS standards follow karni chahiye.
  • Project completion se pehle advance zyada mat lena — 30% max recommended.

⚙️ Equipment Rental — ଯ.

  • Equipment owner ko maintenance records rakhne chahiye.
  • Renter se security deposit lena allowed hai — receipt mandatory.
  • Equipment damage hone par renter responsible — before/after photos lo.
  • Heavy machinery (JCB, Crane) ke liye operator license compulsory.

🚗 Vehicle Booking — ଯ. ବ.

  • Driver ke paas valid license hona chahiye (Motor Vehicles Act 1988).
  • Vehicle insurance mandatory — check karo passenger insurance bhi ho.
  • Goods transport ke liye: vehicle fitness certificate + permit required.
  • Drunk driving = permanent ban + police complaint.
  • Night travel (10PM-5AM) ke liye customer ki written consent (WhatsApp) leni chahiye.

🍽️ Food Delivery — ଖ. ଡ.

  • Food vendors ko FSSAI registration/license hona chahiye (Food Safety & Standards Act 2006).
  • Home food makers ke liye FSSAI basic registration (free, online) karo.
  • Allergens: Customer ko batana mandatory — dairy, nuts, gluten etc.
  • Food delivery 2 ghante mein honi chahiye nahi toh fresh nahi maana jayega.
  • Food poisoning complaint mein vendor ko full refund dena hoga.

🛒 Marketplace Buy/Sell — ବ.

  • Seller ko item ki sahi condition batani chahiye — misleading description = ban.
  • Second-hand electronics: 7-day return policy recommended.
  • Land/property listing: Documents verify karo pehle — fraud hone par IPC 420 lagu.
  • Animals sale: Vaccination records mandatory. Stolen animals list karna IPC 379.
  • Vegetables/crops: Fresh hone chahiye — galat quality pe complaint hogi.

🏪 Sunday Haata — ରବ. ହ.

  • Haata mein sirf genuine local products — fake/duplicate products ban.
  • Handloom: GI (Geographical Indication) tags respect karo — Sambalpuri/Nuapatna designs.
  • Wild animals ya protected birds sell karna Wildlife Protection Act 1972 ke under illegal.
  • Haata listing 7 din ke liye active rahegi — uske baad renew karo.

🪪 Digital Work ID — ଡ. କ. ID

  • Work ID sirf genuine workers ke liye hai — misuse hone par cancel hoga.
  • Work ID government ID ka replacement nahi hai — sirf platform identity proof hai.
  • QR code link kisi ke saath bhi share karo — lekin account password share mat karo.
⭐ Dual Rating System: Is platform pe dono sides rate kar sakte hain — Customer worker ko aur Worker customer ko. Ye rural trust build karta hai. — ଦ. ।

⭐ Customer → Worker Rating

  1. Booking complete hone ke baad customer 5-star rating de sakta hai.
  2. Rating 1 se 5 stars mein — 5 best, 1 worst.
  3. Written feedback optional hai lekin encouraged hai.
  4. Quick tags se bhi feedback de sakte hain (Sahi kaam, Samay pe aaye, etc.)
  5. Rating published hogi worker ke public profile pe.
  6. Fake/malicious rating report karo — 24hr mein review hogi.

⭐ Worker → Customer Rating

  1. Worker bhi customer ko rate kar sakta hai apne orders section se.
  2. Customer rating visible hogi future workers ko — taaki they know who is reliable.
  3. Worker tags: "Sahi payment diya", "Kaam clearly bataya", "Respectful", etc.
  4. Low rated customers ko platform alert de sakta hai.
  5. Kisi ne agar 3 se kam rating di repeatedly — platform review karega.

📝 Feedback Guidelines

  • Feedback mein personal attacks nahi — sirf kaam ke baare mein.
  • Rating ke baad 72 ghante mein edit nahi ho sakti.
  • Dono parties rating de sakte hain same order pe.
  • 3 se kam rating pe admin ko auto-alert milega.
  • Galat rating report karna: WhatsApp pe screenshot bhejo.
