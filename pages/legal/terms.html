📋 Terms & Conditions

Welcome to The Part-Timers — Odisha's rural services platform.

  • Workers keep 100% of their earnings. Platform charges 0% commission.
  • Vendor subscriptions fund platform operations and maintenance.
  • All users must provide accurate, truthful information when registering.
  • Platform reserves the right to suspend abusive accounts immediately.
  • Bookings are direct agreements between customers and workers — not with the platform.
  • Platform acts only as a marketplace connector, not an employer.
  • Language: English and Odia only on this platform.
  • Disputes between workers and customers must be resolved between themselves.
  • Fake profiles or impersonation will result in permanent ban.

🔐 Privacy Policy

  • We collect only basic information needed to operate the platform (name, phone, village).
  • Phone numbers are shown only to confirmed booking parties.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • Location data is used only to show nearby services.
  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.
  • We use localStorage for saving preferences — no server-side account data without Google Sheets integration.

💰 Payment & Booking Policy

  • All payments are made directly to workers — not through the platform.
  • Platform charges a 5% facilitation fee only on confirmed bookings with Sheets integration.
  • No advance payment is taken through this platform in demo mode.
  • Cancellations must be communicated to the worker directly via phone/WhatsApp.
  • Refund disputes are between the customer and worker — platform is not liable.

👷 Worker Policy

  • Workers must provide accurate skill information and availability.
  • Workers are responsible for quality and timely completion of their services.
  • Overcharging beyond agreed rates is a violation and will lead to profile removal.
  • Workers must maintain professional behaviour with customers.
  • Verified badge is earned by providing valid ID and maintaining 4.0+ rating.
